# Proposed 7‑digit updates to the SALI's industry taxonomy (aligned to V2 SALI & 2022 NAICS)

Below are “parent” V2 SALI & 2022 NAICS codes with recommended 7‑digit extensions.

A. Securities, funds, and investment management

- 523940 Portfolio Management and Investment Advice   
  - 5239401 Private Equity/Buyout Advisors (incl. growth equity)  
  - 5239402 Venture Capital Advisors (seed, early, multi‑stage)  
  - 5239403 Hedge Fund Advisors (equity L/S, macro, quant, multi‑PM)  
  - 5239404 Private Credit/Direct Lending Advisors (BDCs, specialty finance)  
  - 5239405 Real Assets/Real Estate/Infrastructure Advisors  
  - 5239406 Fund of Funds and OCIOs  
  - 5239407 Family Offices (SFO/MFO) and Wealth Managers  
  - 5239408 Digital/Robo‑Advisors  
  - 5239409 SMA/UMA/Wrap Program Managers

- 523150 Investment Banking and Securities Intermediation 
  - 5231501 Retail Brokerage/Wealth Platforms  
  - 5231502 Institutional Brokerage/Prime Brokerage  
  - 5231503 Introducing Brokers  
  - 5231504 Digital/Crypto Brokerages  
    - Note: Some crypto intermediation models may instead fit 523910 Miscellaneous Intermediation (case‑by‑case) 

B. Technology, data, media, telecom

- 513210 Software Publishers   
  - 5132101 Horizontal SaaS (ERP/CRM/HR/FinOps)  
  - 5132102 Vertical SaaS (health, fintech, govtech, legaltech)  
  - 5132103 Cybersecurity Software/Platforms  
  - 5132104 Data/Analytics/AI Platforms (incl. foundation models)  
  - 5132105 Developer Tools/DevOps  
  - 5132106 Gaming/Interactive Entertainment  
  - 5132107 AdTech/MarTech

- 518210 Computing Infrastructure Providers, Data Processing, Web Hosting, and Related Services   
  - 5182101 IaaS/PaaS Cloud Providers  
  - 5182102 Colocation/Data Center Operators  
  - 5182103 CDN/Edge Networks  
  - 5182104 Managed Hosting/MSPs  
  - 5182105 Crypto Mining/Staking Data Centers

- 516210 Media Streaming Distribution Services, Social Networks, and Other Media Networks and Content Providers — for social/UGC and OTT/streaming   
  - 5162101 Social Media/UGC Platforms  
  - 5162102 Streaming/OTT Platforms  
  - 5162103 Creator Economy/Influencer Platforms  
- 519290 Web Search Portals and All Other Information Services — for search portals and multi‑sided marketplaces as platforms   
  - 5192901 Online Marketplaces (multi‑sided platforms)

- AI companies (e.g., OpenAI) — classify under 513210 Software Publishers; tag with 5132104 Data/Analytics/AI Platforms (foundation models) 

C. Life sciences and healthcare

- 32541x Pharmaceutical and Medicine Manufacturing  
  - 325411 Medicinal and Botanical Manufacturing  
  - 325412 Pharmaceutical Preparation Manufacturing  
    - 3254121 Small‑Molecule (innovator)  
    - 3254122 Small‑Molecule (generic)  
  - 325413 In‑Vitro Diagnostic Substance Manufacturing  
  - 325414 Biological Product (except Diagnostic) Manufacturing  
    - 3254141 Biologics (incl. biosimilars)  
    - 3254142 Cell & Gene Therapy  

- 541714/541715 Research and Development in Biotechnology/Life Sciences — valid in 2022 (nanotechnology remains 541713)   
  - 5417141 Preclinical Biotech  
  - 5417142 Clinical‑Stage Biotech  
  

- 339112 Surgical and Medical Instrument Manufacturing — current; use for device companies (cross‑reference 334510 as needed)   
  - 3391121 Class II Devices  
  - 3391122 Class III/Implantables  
  - 3391123 Diagnostics (IVD) [coordinate with 325413 if reagent‑centric]  
  - 3391124 SaMD/Digital Therapeutics

D. Real estate, construction, infrastructure

- 531110 Lessors of Residential Buildings and Dwelling  
  - 5311101 Multifamily  
  - 5311102 Single‑Family Rental/Build‑to‑Rent  
  - 5311103 Student Housing  
  - 5311104 Senior Housing/CCRC
- 531120 Lessors of Nonresidential Buildings (except Miniwarehouses)
  - 5311201 Industrial/Logistics  
  - 5311202 Office  
  - 5311203 Retail  
  - 5311204 Life Sciences/Lab  
  - 5311205 Data Centers

- Add explicit parents for two asset types you listed as “x06/x07”:  
  - 531130 Lessors of Miniwarehouses and Self‑Storage Units (Self‑Storage)   
  - 531190 Lessors of Other Real Estate Property (Manufactured Housing Communities commonly tagged here) 

E. Cannabis (cross‑mapped; jurisdiction‑dependent)

- Cultivation: 111419 Other Food Crops Grown Under Cover or 111998 All Other Miscellaneous Crop Farming (indoor vs. other methods)    
- Processing/Manufacturing: 325411 Medicinal and Botanical Manufacturing (extraction); food/beverage infusions use the appropriate 311/312 code (e.g., 312120 Breweries only as an example for beverages)   
- Wholesale Distribution: 424590 Other Farm Product Raw Material Merchant Wholesalers or 424990 Other Miscellaneous Nondurable Goods Merchant Wholesalers   
- Retail/Dispensaries: 459999 All Other Miscellaneous Retailers (neutral retail placement)   
- Ancillary Services: map to 54xxxx by activity (e.g., 541519 Other Computer Related Services; 541613 Marketing Consulting)
